My top 4 artists/groups
Eveline Mendu
Last updated Sept 22, 2022
1.
Queen
Rock Glamrock Hardrock

Queen is an English rock band. The band was formed in 1970 in London by guitarist Brian May, singer Freddie Mercury and drummer Roger Taylor, joined by bassist John Deacon in 1971. With dozens of hits in the 1970s, 1980s and 1990s, Queen is one of the most successful rock bands in history.

My Favorite songs: Bohemian Rhapsody, Keep Yourself Alive, Don't Stop Me Now, Bicycle Race

2.
Michael Jackson
R&b, rock, soul, dance, pop

Michael Joseph Jackson also know as 'The King Of Pop' was an American singer, dancer, composer and philanthropist. He is certified and known as the most awarded and nominated recording artist in the history of popular music. Michael Jackson is considered to be the greatest entertainer of all time and the most influential artist of the 20th century.

I love Michael Jackson for the entertainer he was, for the musical genius he was. There will never be a male performer like him again.

My Favorite songs: Baby Be Mine, Chicago, They Don't Care About Us, Human Nature, P.Y.T.

3.
Rihanna
Barbadian pop, R&B

Rihanna became a worldwide star in the early 21st century, known for her distinctive and versatile voice and for her fashionable appearance. I don't even need to introduce her. Not only do I think Rihanna's music is super good, but I have enormous respect for her as a person. She endured a difficult childhood. From zero to hero as the saying goes. But despite her difficult childhood, she managed to become a world star. She started her own brand in 2017 which earned her the billionaire status she carries with her today.

My Favorite songs: Vanilla Bean Latte

4.
Giveon
R&B, soul

Giveon Dezmann Evans is an American singer-songwriter. He rose to prominence with his collaboration with Drake on their 2020 single, "Chicago Freestyle". He is very well known among the youth. Everyone who listens to him says the same thing about him: He has a voice that you don't get tired of, you can spend hours and hours listening to his songs. In his songs he takes you through the rollercoaster he faces every dahg. As he said before "my life is an emotional rollercoaster". He gained even more popularity earlier this year when he lauched his newest album 'Give or Take'. It reached numer one in the charts and that's because there's not one bad song on it. Don't believe me? Go listen, NOW! I like to refer to 'Give or Take' a blessing for the ears.

My Favorite songs: Lie Again, Scarred, World We Created, Fields, For Tonight
